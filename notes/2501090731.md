# [title:: 中轴数]

[中轴数](../concepts/midhinge.md) 即第一个和第三个 [四分位数](../concepts/quartile.md) 的 [算术平均值](../concepts/arithmetic_mean.md)。

$$
MH(X) = \overline {Q_{{1 \over 4},{3 \over 4}}(X)} = {\frac {Q_{1 \over 4}(X) + Q_{3 \over 4}(X)}{2}}
$$
